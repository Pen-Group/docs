Looks Blocks

"set colour to [colour]"

Sets the colour of the current pixel, or vertex to the desired colour.

"colour"

Returns the current colour.

"set [attribute] to [value]" and "change [attribute] by [value]"

Sets or changes the desired attribute to or by the desired value.
Attributes are

  • hue (0-360)
  • saturation (0-100)
  • value (0-100)
  • red (0-255)
  • green (0-255)
  • blue (0-255)
  • alpha (0-255)

"apply multiplicative blending"

Makes transparency blend instead of brighten pixels behind it.
