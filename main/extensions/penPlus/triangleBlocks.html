Triangle Blocks

If you are looking for list based rendering check list blocks

This is where 3d Gurus get excited. The triangle blocks contain many helpful utilities for drawing triangles.
Though this set of blocks only exists for singular triangles.

"set triangle point [point]'s [attribute] to [value]"

Some attributes only effect textured triangles. Ones with a (T) at the beginning are those ones.

This block sets the attribute of the desired point on the triangle to the value desired. Even individual components of tints can be changed.

The different attributes include.

  • (T)U value - The horizontal point of the texture this point of the triangle samples from. UV's are a 0-1 range!
  • (T)V value - The vertical point of the texture this point of the triangle samples from. UV's are a 0-1 range!
  • Red Tint - Changes the amount of red that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Green Tint - Changes the amount of green that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Blue Tint - Changes the amount of blue that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Transparency - Sets the transparency of the point will be interpolated to the other points.
  • Corner Pinch - Squishes the attributes of a point more to that corner relative to the other points.
  • Depth Value - Sets the depth in which the point will be rendered. Good for layering, or 3d effects.


"set triangle's [attribute] to [value]"

This block sets the attributes of a whole triangle more limited than setting each point individually.

  • Red Tint - Changes the amount of red that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Green Tint - Changes the amount of green that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Blue Tint - Changes the amount of blue that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Transparency - Sets the transparency of the point will be interpolated to the other points.
  • Depth Value - Sets the depth in which the point will be rendered. Good for layering, or 3d effects.


"tint triangle point [id] to [color]"

Tints the point to the desired color.


"tint triangle to [color]"

Tints the whole triangle to the desired color.


"get triangle point [id]'s [attribute]"

Gets the value of a triangle's attribute


"reset triangle attributes"

Resets the attributes of a triangle to their default state.


"draw triangle between [x] [y], [x] [y], and [x] [y]"

Draws a triangle between the three points with your attributes.


"draw textured triangle between [x] [y], [x] [y], and [x] [y] with the texture [texture]"

Draws a triangle between the three points with your attributes. With the texture/costume on it.
