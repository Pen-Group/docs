Square Blocks

Pen Property blocks look into the default pen's properties, or even detach certain functions of the pen.

"stamp pen square"

Stamps a square using the current pen size and attributes with its own attributes.


"stamp pen square with the texture of [texture]"

Stamps a square using the current pen size and attributes with its own attributes. With an image attached!


Some attributes may only work on textured squares, if it has a (T) it is for only textured squares.

"set pen square's [attribute] to [value]"

Sets the attribute of a sprite's pen square! Attributes include

  • Width - a multiplier of the square's width
    The formula would be (pen size) * (square width)
  • Height - a multiplier of the square's height
    The formula would be (pen size) * (square height)
  • Rotation - the rotation of the pen square
  • (T)U-Multiplier - the multiplier for the U coordinates of the pen square
    Think of it as stretching the image horizontally.
  • (T)U-Offset - the offset for the U coordinates of the pen square
    Think of it as scrolling the image to the left or right.
  • (T)V-Multiplier - the multiplier for the V coordinates of the pen square
    Think of it as stretching the image vertically.
  • (T)V-Offset - the offset for the V coordinates of the pen square.
    Think of it as scrolling the image to the up or down.
  • Red Tint - Changes the amount of red that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Green Tint - Changes the amount of green that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Blue Tint - Changes the amount of blue that shows through a 1-100 range.
  • Transparency - Sets the transparency of the square
  • Depth Value - Sets the depth in which the square will be rendered. Good for layering.
